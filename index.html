[01] ABOUT

We don't throw
parties.
We build
experiences.

Established in 2026, Barcode Bombay is an unapologetic events collective dedicated entirely to supporting and amplifying Bombay's underground artists. We curate intimate, high-fidelity audio experiences in undisclosed venues across the city. No generic headliners. No egos. Just sound.

Volume 1 marks our inception. Designed fundamentally from the ground up — from the brutalist lighting rigs to the speaker arrays — to build a foundation that puts the underground artists first.
